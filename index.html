About Me

Greetings! My name is Arsh, and I find immense joy in creating solutions that simplify people's lives. Originally from Punjab, India, I moved to Canada in 2019 to pursue a Bachelor's degree in Science. My primary area of interest is machine learning, with a special focus on deep learning and computer vision.

My journey into the world of computers began at a very young age. When I was just six years old, my father bought our first computer. It ran on an older version of Windows XP, and I spent countless hours exploring the operating system and playing with simulation software. My curiosity often led me to create impossible simulations, blending my interest in physics with my newfound love for technology.

As I grew older, I started writing small bash scripts to automate tasks on my computer. Whenever a computer broke in our neighborhood, I was the go-to person for repairs. It was no surprise to my family when I decided to pursue a career in software engineering. After grade 12, I taught myself C++, fascinated by its power to create anything.

Over time, I have become proficient in other programming languages such as Java, JavaScript, and Python. I'm a quick learner, able to pick up new technologies swiftly. My skills span across web development, mobile development, data science, and low-level programming, with a particular interest in parallel programming and machine learning.
