About Me

Greetings! My name is Arsh, and I find great joy in creating solutions that make people's lives easier. Originally from Punjab, India, I made my way to Canada in 2019 to pursue a Bachelor's degree in Science. My primary area of interest is low-level programming, which I've been exploring since the tender age of eight when I first tinkered with HTML code. Since then, my curiosity about computer hardware has led me to write bash scripts, repair computers, and eventually dive into programming with C/C++.

Over time, I've also become proficient in other programming languages such as Java, JavaScript, and Python.

In addition to low-level programming, I also have experience with web development, mobile development and data science. I have a particular interest in Parallel Programming and Machine Learning.
